Winter Cider #1
by Jeff Summit, summit_j@orgella.com


Day 1:  8 @ 12oz apple juice concentrate
        8 quarts water
        1 cup light brown sugar


        SG = 1.050
        acid = 0.5% tartaric


        heat to 150 F
        let cool to 100 F


        add 14g Whitbread yeast
        transfer to primary fermenter


Day 2:  keep at 70 F with thermastat & lamp in a box in my basement
        SG = 1.030


Day 21: SG = 1.000
        Rack into 3 gallon carboy, add air lock
        Top off with 1 quart applejuice & 1 quart water


Day 26: 70 F and active, turn off heating lamp


Day 27: 58 F and no activity


Day 36: 60 F and slight activity


Day 57: 60 F, slight activity and clear


Day 81: 60 F, almost no activity
        rack into a clean vessel
        add 1 cup corn sugar and bottle


Day 150: tastes bitter, light carbonation & alchoholic


Day 185: tastes good, mellow, high carbonation
