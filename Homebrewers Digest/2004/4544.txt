Date: Tue, 15 Jun 2004 21:07:56 -0400
From: "Mark E.  Hogenmiller" <mehogenmiller at cox.net>
Subject: "Spirit of Free Beer" Homebrew Competition Results


Brewers United For Real Potables  (BURP) completed the 12th Annual Nation's
Capital  "Spirit of Free Beer" Homebrew Competition at the Old Dominion
Brewery at Ashburn, Virginia on June 12, 2004.  Spirit of Free Beer, for the
sixth year in a row, served as one of the Qualifying Events for the Masters
Championship for Amateur Brewing (MCAB).  BURP would like to especially
thank the 45 BJCP judges, 2 professional brewers and 1 novice judge who
dedicated their services to judging 332 entries from AK, IL, MD, NC, NH, NJ,
NY, OR, PA, TN, TX, VA making this truly a signature/showcase event.


The Best of show winners are:
1st Place                   Andy Anderson                   Club: BURP
Brown Ale - 10A Mild          "Who stole my hops?"


2nd Place                   Mark Hogenmiller                Club: BURP
European Dark Lagers - 13B Schwarzbier       "Black Tiger"


3rd Place                 Michael Robinson               Club: Boston Wort
Processors
German Amber Lager - 9A  Oktoberfest/Maerzen      "Octoberfest"


The winner of the Bill Moe Memorial Extract Award for the highest placing
extract beer is:


Mike Heniff         Club: FOAM Rangers (TX)
Specialty/Experimental/Historical Beer - 24      "81 Baltic Porter"


Complete results will be posted to BURP website shortly at
http://www.burp.org/events/sofb/2004/ .  BURP would also like to thank all
our sponsors and encourage all home brewers to visit the sponsor page at
http://www.burp.org/events/sofb/2004/sponsors.asp


SOFB XII Team
events at burp.org
