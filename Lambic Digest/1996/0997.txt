***********"THE BEERS OF WALLONIA - BELGIUM'S BEST KEPT SECRET"***************
by John Woods and Keith Rigley.


This new hard-back book (176 pp.) includes:-


  -  descriptions of all 36 Wallonian breweries: their beers, tasting notes,
     and availability;
  -  describes a day brewing at A Vapeur Brewery;

  -  lavish illustrations -  maps, beer mats, labels, etc.
