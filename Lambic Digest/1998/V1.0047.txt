>From: Nathan_L_Kanous_Ii at ferris.edu
>Date: Tue, 10 Mar 1998 08:05:20 -0500
>Subject: Hops for pLambic


Just buy old hops at a coop or natural foods market.  They are usually old.
 Old hops depending on the variety really won't impart much bitternes from
alpha acids but can impart some from the oxidized beta acids. Or just save
some hops used from a previous batch and wash them and dry them.  Then you
will have debittered filter aid.
